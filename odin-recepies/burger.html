Burger

Description

A classic juicy burger.

Ingredients

  • Ground chicken patties
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Ketchup

How to make? Let's begin!

  1. Grill the chicken patties until fully cooked.
  2. Toast the burger buns.
  3. Assemble the burger with toppings.
